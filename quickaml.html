The QuicKaml Project

Etymology

It's an overlap of Quick C(K)aml, a parody to Objective Caml (OCaml). Interestingly, though regarded as a member of ML family, the suffix doesn't share the same meaning. It stands for Machine Language in Caml, instead of Meta Language. The name shows my wish to have a quick language in ML-style. Oh, it would be a hard task.
